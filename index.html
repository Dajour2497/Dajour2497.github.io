About Me

I’m thrilled you’re here—because I have so much I want to share with you. Through this blog, I will share my journey and story as I follow God, live out His will, and exercise my faith in every area of my life - well, prayerfully (LOL). I want to show that God can be and wants to be a part of every aspect of our lives from self-care to travel and everything in between! I hope to inspire others and bring unity to our diversity as Christians by sharing diverse stories and God-inspired thoughts.
